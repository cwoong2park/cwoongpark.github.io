Spatial Influence of ICT - Digitally Accessible Space

    Enhancing the Disaster Resilience of Declining City

    Since declining city is more likely to be vulnerable to natural disaster, the risk assessment of the site is required with multi-dimensional approach. Specifically, this project focused on the risk assessment of a small area, analyzing based on 1m scale grid. I addressed climate data which represent the hazard factor based on IPCC 2014 framework and obtained 3D point cloud data using drone & LiDAR.

  • Acquire drone flight certificate
  • Park, C. W., Byun, G., Lee, H. K., Jang, K. M., No, W., & Kim, Y. (2020). Development of a Risk Assessment Model of Rainfall for Small Area in Declining Urban Areas. KIEAE Journal, 20(6), 7-12.

    EDISON - Web based Urban Design Simulator

    EDucation-research Integration through Simulation On the NET (EDISON) is educational web-solver for engineering students. In order to provide web-based urban design simulation, I mainly developed fundamental solver which calculates how much land is required to fit the demand based on population, density, land use, and FAR.     (Ongoing) Clustering Subway Pattern

    Wavelet transformation enables encapsulate temporal data to two sets of numbers which represents the signal pattern. Since it can be applied every time-wise data, I transformed the subway pattern into set of coefficients which can be clustered using wavelet transformation. Based on the clustering, urban characteristics and people's travel behavior can be analyzed with public transportation pattern.

    Simulating Water Flow on Facade

    I conducted research for the simulation of water flow pattern on facade during my internship in VS-A.kr. A real experiment was conducted to understand the water flow pattern depending on the material and angle. Based on the experiment, the pattern was parametrized by CFD simulation (Realflow) and visualized by 3D modeling tool (C#, Rhino3D & Grasshopper).

  • Park, C. W., Park, J., Kim, N., & Kim, Y. (2018). Modeling water flow on Façade. Automation in Construction, 93, 265-279.
